# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

Be considerate, assume good intent, focus criticism on the work, and do not publish another person's private information. Harassment, discrimination, threats, and abusive behavior are not acceptable.

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate these expectations and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's maintainer contact or GitHub moderation tools.
